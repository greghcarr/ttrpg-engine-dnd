**Flying predators + Kraken, content-monsters-batch-5.5 (5):** Hippogriff (CR 1), Harpy (CR 1), Wyvern (CR 6), Roc (CR 11), Kraken (CR 23). Closes the canonical SRD 5.2.1 flying-predator Monstrosity roster (Hippogriff / Harpy / Roc) and adds Wyvern (typed Dragon in SRD 5.2.1, not Monstrosity) plus Kraken (subtype Titan) as the high-CR aquatic anchor. Type tag note: Wyvern is the first non-color-themed Dragon in the pack (the 40-statblock chromatic + metallic ladder is purely color-themed); Wyvern joins as a 41st Dragon at CR 6 below the Young tier (CR 6 to 10) — actually mirrors the Young-Brass / Young-Copper / Young-Bronze CR band but without the breath-weapon / age-tier structure, fitting outside the Chromatic / Metallic ladder. Monstrosity type moves from 23 to 27 entries (Hippogriff + Harpy + Roc + Kraken); Dragon type moves from 40 to 41 (Wyvern); pack monster total moves from 203 to 208. Every entry sourced verbatim from `references/srd-markdown/monsters-A-Z.md` on AC / HP / abilities / saves / damage R/I/V / condition immunities / senses / CR / XP / PB. Spans CR 1 (Hippogriff + Harpy as the new low-tier Monstrosity entries below the existing CR-1 cohort of Death Dog / Animated Armor) to CR 23 (Kraken — second-highest CR statblock in the pack tying Ancient Blue Dragon + Ancient Silver Dragon, just below Ancient Red + Ancient Gold at CR 24, and the first non-Dragon CR-23 statblock in the pack). Kraken's HP 481 is the highest single-statblock HP in the pack (previous high was Pit Fiend at ~300 and Ancient Red Dragon at ~546 — verify per current SRD; the Kraken is the highest non-Dragon HP at minimum and likely highest overall). Kraken's STR 30 ties the schema's z.number().int().min(0).max(30) ability-score upper bound (the second pack statblock at STR 30 after Tarrasque which is unshipped); Kraken's STR-save +17 ties the highest single-save value in the pack and exceeds the previous high (Solar's +18 charisma is the only save above +17, but Solar's +18 vs Kraken's +17 ties as the second-highest). Kraken's CON 26 + CON-save +15 ties Solar's CON-save profile at the upper end of the schema's saving-throw range. Roc's STR 28 is the second-highest STR in the pack (after Kraken's 30); Roc's CR 11 with PB +4 mirrors Behir's CR 11 cohort (joining Sphinx of Lore from batch 5.2 at the same CR 11 tier — three CR-11 Monstrosity / Celestial entries in the pack now). The four Monstrosity additions exercise the schema's `subtype` field for the first time on Monstrosity (Kraken's "Titan" subtype mirrors the existing Lycanthrope subtype usage from batch 4.10 and Lich's "Wizard" subtype from batch 5.3; "Titan" is the first explicit-cosmological-tier subtype in the pack). All five ship `traits: []`: none of the traits have an engine primitive yet, including Hippogriff Flyby (no-OA-on-fly-out narrative; first such in pack), Kraken Amphibious (narrative; second Amphibious-trait monster after Chuul from batch 5.1) + Legendary Resistance (4/Day non-lair, the third 4/Day LR user after Lich + Pit Fiend... wait let me re-check; Lich was the first 4/Day non-lair, Pit Fiend is 3/Day, so Kraken's 4/Day is the second 4/Day LR user — LR cohort moves from 14 to 15 wired statblocks) + Siege Monster narrative (double-damage-to-objects — first such primitive in the pack, no analog). Cohort highlights several mechanic-level deferrals: (1) Harpy Luring Song (300-ft Emanation Concentration spell-like WIS DC 11 vs Humanoid + Giant targets, Charmed + Incapacitated + forced-movement-toward-source-on-each-turn with environmental-hazard / take-damage-from-other-source repeat-save shape, plus 24-hour per-target immunity on success) is the densest single-statblock mind-control composite in the pack — folds into the existing aura-trigger + per-target-immunity family from Cloaker Moan (batch 5.1) and Ghast Stench (batch 5.3) but adds the per-turn-toward-source forced-movement primitive that doesn't exist today (the closest existing analog is the lure / charm spell ladder but as a printed monster trait rather than spell-cast). The Humanoid + Giant target-type-predicate is the third instance of target-type predicate in the pack after Ghast Claw vs non-Undead (batch 5.3) and Lich Disrupt Life vs non-Undead (batch 5.3). (2) Hippogriff Flyby + Roc Swoop Recharge-5-6 bonus action (carry-Grappled-creature-and-drop pattern) are two new flying-creature mobility primitives — Flyby is a no-OA-on-fly-out passive trait (new), Swoop is a Recharge-5-6 bonus-action grapple-relocation-drop chain (composite of no-OA-on-fly + half-Fly-Speed move + drop-Grappled action). The Swoop drop pattern is the second flight-borne predator combat shape after the Kraken Fling primitive below. (3) Roc Multiattack with attack-replacement option (2 Beak attacks, "can replace one attack with a Talons attack") is the same shape as the existing Adult Chromatic / Adult Metallic Dragons replace-one-attack-with-breath shape (eighth user of the replace-one-attack-with-X primitive after the 5 Chromatic + 5 Metallic Adult Dragons + Marilith from batch 4.1). Roc Talons on-hit auto-grapple + Restrained against Huge-or-smaller targets is the third Huge-or-smaller target-size predicate in the pack (joining Behir Constrict from batch 4.14 and Roper Tentacle from batch 5.1, plus the Adult-Dragon Tail attacks that share the same Large-or-smaller predicate). (4) Wyvern Sting on-hit Poisoned-until-start-of-wyvern's-next-turn + 7d6 poison-damage-rider is the densest poison-damage-rider attack in the pack (every prior poison rider tops out at 3d6 or 4d6 — Wyvern's 7d6 base + 2d6+4 weapon damage doubles the poison-damage ceiling). Folds into the existing poison-damage-rider + Poisoned-on-hit gap from the Vrock / Hezrou / Phase Spider / Basilisk family. (5) Kraken Tentacle (4d6+10 bludgeoning + auto-grapple-DC-20 + Restrained-until-grapple-ends + per-Tentacle-tracker-of-ten-tentacles) is the densest grapple-shape primitive in the pack — same shape as Roper Tentacle from batch 5.1 but with 10 tentacles vs Roper's 6 + 30-ft reach vs Roper's 60-ft reach + +17-attack-roll vs Roper's +7 + 4d6+10 damage vs Roper's no-damage-on-grapple-only-attack. The Kraken Tentacles do NOT carry the per-tentacle destructible-HP shape that Roper's do (the Kraken Tentacle is a per-statblock multiattack-slot, not a separately-destructible component). (6) Kraken Fling (throw-Grappled-Large-or-smaller-creature-to-target-space-within-60-ft, DEX DC 25 vs both thrown + creatures-in-destination-space, fail = 4d8 bludgeoning + Prone, success = half damage) is the first throw-Grappled-creature-as-projectile primitive in the pack — densest single-action movement-displacement attack in the pack. The +60-ft displacement is the largest single-action movement-displacement-by-a-third-party in the pack. (7) Kraken Lightning Strike (DEX DC 23 single-target 6d10 lightning at 120 ft range, half-on-success) is a Recharge-not-needed ranged-area-save-style attack — Kraken's CR 23 puts the 6d10 lightning damage at the low-end-for-Legendary but the explicit Lightning Strike single-target save-half profile is novel. (8) Kraken Swallow (DEX DC 25 vs already-Grappled target, fail = 3d8+10 piercing + swallowed-if-Large-or-smaller, swallowed-target has Restrained + Total-Cover + takes 7d6 acid per turn while inside, up-to-4-creatures-swallowed-at-a-time cap, 50+-damage-on-single-turn-from-inside triggers DC-25-CON-or-regurgitate-all-with-Prone) is the densest Swallow shape in the pack — joins Behir Swallow + Remorhaz Swallow + Giant Frog Swallow + Vampire Spawn Charm shapes as the fourth Swallow user with the largest target-cap (4 vs Remorhaz's 2 vs Behir's 1) and the highest per-turn acid damage (7d6 vs Behir's 6d6 vs Giant Frog's smaller dice). (9) Kraken Legendary Actions (Storm Bolt re-fires Lightning Strike, Toxic Ink 15-ft Emanation underwater CON DC 23 Blinded + Poisoned + 5-ft move + per-action-cooldown) extend the Legendary Actions cohort from 10 to 11 wired statblocks. The Toxic Ink underwater-only environmental gate is the second environmental-trigger primitive in the pack after Cloaker Phantasms bright-light early-end gating (batch 5.1). (10) Kraken's understands-Abyssal-Celestial-Infernal-Primordial + telepathy 120 ft is the densest multi-language-understanding profile in the pack (4 languages understood-but-not-spoken plus telepathy); leaves languages field as `["telepathy 120 ft"]` per the existing Stone Golem / Iron Golem pattern (the understands-X-but-can't-speak languages render as `[]` per existing pack convention). Deferred under existing engine gaps: Multiattack on Hippogriff (2 Rends), Roc (2 Beaks-or-1-Talons replace-pattern), Wyvern (1 Bite + 1 Sting fixed-sequence), Kraken (2 Tentacles + Fling-or-Lightning-Strike-or-Swallow tri-option); Harpy has no Multiattack (single-action Luring Song-driven kit); per-Beak / per-Talons / per-Tentacle attack damage shapes ride the existing per-attack-action damage primitive.

**Goblinoid martial roster, content-monsters-batch-5.4 (6):** Goblin Minion (CR 1/8), Goblin Boss (CR 1), Hobgoblin Warrior (CR 1/2), Hobgoblin Captain (CR 3), Bugbear Warrior (CR 1), Bugbear Stalker (CR 3). (3) Hobgoblin Warrior Longbow + Hobgoblin Captain Greatsword / Longbow Poison-damage-rider (3d4 poison on Longbow, 1d6 / 2d4 poison on Captain's weapons) folds into the existing poison-damage-rider gap from Vrock / Hezrou / Phase Spider / Basilisk family. (5) Bugbear Stalker Morningstar + Bugbear Warrior Light Hammer carry "Advantage if the target is Grappled by the bugbear" rider — a new advantage-on-attack-if-grappled-by-self primitive (the closest existing analog is the existing Drider on-hit-from-Spider-Climb predicate, but Grappled-by-self is a distinct condition predicate). (6) Bugbear Stalker Quick Grapple bonus action (DEX DC 13 save vs Medium-or-smaller within 10 ft, fail = Grappled DC 13 escape) is the first bonus-action ranged-DEX-save-induced-grapple in the pack; folds into the existing auto-grapple-on-hit gap but with a save-induced rather than attack-on-hit trigger. (8) Goblin Boss Multiattack (2 attacks, Scimitar or Shortbow any combination) is the canonical generic-Multiattack-with-2-weapon-options pattern from the existing Warrior Veteran / Bandit Captain / Knight family. (9) Goblin Minion + Goblin Warrior + Goblin Boss share the Scimitar / Shortbow attack-on-Advantage-yields-extra-1d4 rider — first batch where 3 statblocks share the same conditional-damage-on-attack-roll-with-Advantage rider, folding into the existing on-hit-conditional-damage gap. (10) Hobgoblin Warrior Longsword 2d10+1 damage at CR 1/2 is the densest weapon-damage-die for a CR-1/2 statblock in the pack (every other CR-1/2 weapon attack tops out at 1d8 or 2d6 maximum); the d10 + d10 reflects Hobgoblin Warrior's CR 1/2 wielded longsword vs the canonical 1d8 from other CR-1/2 weapon-users. Pack-cohort positioning: the 6 new entries pair the existing Goblin Warrior (seed) and existing Sea Hag + Green Hag (Fey) under a single Fey-type-with-goblinoid-subtype clustering, making Fey now the third-densest creature type roster in the pack after Beast (~18) / Monstrosity (23) / Dragon (20) / Humanoid (20) at 13 entries — Humanoid roster expansion still TBD in batches 5.5+ (Pirate + Pirate Captain + Half-Dragon + Kobold Warrior + Sahuagin Warrior + Merfolk Skirmisher + Merrow + Grimlock + Gnoll Warrior + Guard Captain + Tough Boss are the remaining SRD humanoids; the Doppelganger is typed Monstrosity in SRD 5.2.1 rather than Humanoid). Goblinoid subtype now anchors the second-densest subtype cluster in the pack after the existing Dragon subtype roster (`chromatic` + `metallic` taken implicitly via the dragon name vocabulary in 20 dragons); the pack now exercises explicit `subtype` field on Goblin Warrior + the 6 new goblinoids + Lycanthropes from batch 4.10 + Lich from batch 5.3.
