# Monster gaps: deferred mechanics catalog (per-RAW-trait gap list)

Catalog of RAW monster mechanics that ship as `traits: []` stubs on individual statblocks today, organized per-mechanic with the statblocks that need each. Extracted from [starter-pack-gaps.md](starter-pack-gaps.md) in slice 249.

This window covers the per-mechanic deferral list: Multiattack, Parry reaction, Cunning Action / Sneak Attack / Evasion, RAW spellcasting, Keen senses family, Dark Devotion / Brave, Reckless, Pack Tactics, Leadership, Divine Eminence, Undead Fortitude, Charge / Pounce / Trampling Charge, and many more. Each entry lists which statblocks need the mechanic and what engine primitive would unblock it.

For the batch-by-batch monster authoring history, see the sibling `gaps-monsters-batches-*.md` files. For the priority queue and "Future engine slices" backlog that prioritizes these mechanics by cohort payoff, see [starter-pack-gaps.md](starter-pack-gaps.md).

---

Ninety-nine of the one-hundred-eighteen statblocks ship `traits: []`; the Undead from batch 1.6, three of the five Fiends from batch 1.7, all five Elementals from batch 1.9, and the two Golems from batch 1.12 currently populate trait data (all `GrantResistance` B/P/S nonmagical entries). The RAW traits and actions on all forty-nine statblocks are deferred until the matching engine primitives land:

- **Multiattack** (Bandit Captain, Cultist Fanatic, Scout, Spy, Tough, Warrior Veteran, Knight, Ghoul, Brown Bear, Black Bear, Wight, Dretch, Stone Giant, Frost Giant, Fire Giant, Cloud Giant, Storm Giant, Air Elemental, Earth Elemental, Fire Elemental, Water Elemental, Invisible Stalker, Griffon, Manticore, Owlbear, Animated Armor, Shield Guardian, Stone Golem, Iron Golem, Aboleth, Gibbering Mouther, Otyugh, Satyr, Unicorn, Deva, Planetar, Solar, Shambling Mound, Treant, Young White Dragon, Young Black Dragon, Young Green Dragon, Young Blue Dragon, Adult White Dragon, Adult Black Dragon, Adult Green Dragon, Adult Blue Dragon, Adult Red Dragon, Bearded Devil, Barbed Devil, Chain Devil, Bone Devil, Tyrannosaurus Rex, Gladiator, Assassin, Vrock, Hezrou, Glabrezu, Nalfeshnee, Marilith, Balor, Horned Devil, Erinyes, Ice Devil, Pit Fiend, Black Dragon Wyrmling, Blue Dragon Wyrmling, Green Dragon Wyrmling, Red Dragon Wyrmling, White Dragon Wyrmling, Ancient White Dragon, Ancient Black Dragon, Ancient Green Dragon, Ancient Blue Dragon, Ancient Red Dragon, Young Brass Dragon, Young Copper Dragon, Young Bronze Dragon, Young Silver Dragon, Young Gold Dragon, Adult Brass Dragon, Adult Copper Dragon, Adult Bronze Dragon, Adult Silver Dragon, Adult Gold Dragon, Bronze Dragon Wyrmling, Silver Dragon Wyrmling, Gold Dragon Wyrmling, Ancient Brass Dragon, Ancient Copper Dragon, Ancient Bronze Dragon, Ancient Silver Dragon, Ancient Gold Dragon, Mummy, Mummy Lord, Vampire Familiar, Vampire Spawn, Vampire, Wererat, Werewolf, Wereboar, Weretiger, Werebear, Green Hag, Night Hag, Djinni, Efreeti, Death Dog, Phase Spider, Chimera, Drider, Medusa, Hydra, Behir, Remorhaz) — the engine has no monster-action / multiattack primitive; statblocks can't yet express "this creature makes N attacks per Attack action."
- **Parry reaction** (Bandit Captain, Knight, Gladiator, Erinyes) — reactions ship as dedicated planners today (counterspell / shield / absorb-elements / sanctuary / feather-fall); there's no generic monster-reaction primitive.
- **Cunning Action / Sneak Attack / Evasion** (Spy Sneak Attack + Cunning Action; Assassin Sneak Attack 4d6 + Evasion) — these are wired as class features on the rogue, but the engine doesn't carry a "monster has class-feature shape" cross-link, so the spy / assassin statblocks can't borrow them. Same gap shape; Assassin's Evasion adds a save-result rewrite (success: no damage, fail: half) that lands at the same install point as Legendary Resistance.
- **RAW spellcasting** (Priest Acolyte, Cultist Fanatic, Priest, Druid, Mage) — monsters can't yet carry a spell list. Cantrip and prepared-spell tables across these five NPCs span Wizard L1 → L9 (Mage's Fire Bolt / Mage Armor / Magic Missile / Shield / Detect Magic / Misty Step / Suggestion / Counterspell / Fireball / Fly / Greater Invisibility / Ice Storm / Cone of Cold), Cleric L5 (Priest's Sacred Flame / Cure Wounds / Bless / Spiritual Weapon / Sanctuary), Druid L4 (Druid's Druidcraft / Produce Flame / Shillelagh / Entangle / Speak with Animals / Animal Friendship / Barkskin / Pass without Trace / Call Lightning), and the Cleric L1 starter list for the Priest Acolyte / Cultist tier. The class-side spellcasting machinery doesn't have a monster entry point; once it does, all five wire as pure data.
- **Keen senses family** (Scout's Keen Hearing and Sight; Giant Rat's Keen Smell; Brown Bear's Keen Smell; Dire Wolf's Keen Hearing and Smell; Mastiff's Keen Hearing and Smell; Black Bear's Keen Smell; Cloud Giant's Keen Smell; Griffon's Keen Sight; Owlbear's Keen Sight and Smell; Blink Dog's Keen Hearing and Smell) — needs an advantage-on-perception primitive scoped to specific senses; the engine carries SetAdvantage on rolls but not on sense-modality-specific perception checks.
- **Dark Devotion** (Cultist Fanatic) and **Brave** (Knight, Gladiator) — advantage on saves against specific conditions (Charmed / Frightened on Dark Devotion, Frightened on Brave). The closest existing primitive (`SetAdvantage on:'save:WIS' advantage`) is too broad; a per-condition-saving-throw advantage primitive would close both cleanly.
- **Reckless** (Berserker; Minotaur) — wired as a class feature on the barbarian (Reckless Attack), but monsters can't yet borrow class features. Same gap shape as Spy's Cunning Action / Sneak Attack.
- **Pack Tactics** (Tough, Warrior Infantry, Giant Rat, Dire Wolf, Werewolf) — advantage on attacks when an ally is within 5 ft of the target. Needs a positional-condition-with-advantage primitive; the engine doesn't model positions (consumer territory), so the engine can't gate the advantage without an injected fact.
- **Leadership** (Knight) — recharge-6 short-rest action that grants up to six allies within 30 ft a d4 to attacks and saves for 1 minute. Needs an aura-style action grant paired with a temporary-modifier-with-duration primitive that combines elements of `GrantAura` and the recurring-rider/concentration machinery, neither of which currently composes for a monster action.
- **Divine Eminence** (Priest) — bonus action that consumes a spell slot to add 1d6 radiant per slot level to the next weapon attack this turn. The on-hit-add-damage shape exists (slice 61's `OnEvent + AddDamage + consumeOnTrigger` rider used by the smite cohort), but the trigger entry today is "cast spell X applies a rider," not "spend a slot on a non-spell bonus action to apply the rider." Needs the slot-spend-without-cast primitive plus monster-bonus-action vocabulary to compose.
- **Undead Fortitude** (Zombie) — when reduced to 0 HP by damage other than radiant or a critical hit, the zombie makes a CON save (DC 5 + damage) and drops to 1 HP on success. Closest analogue is slice 111's `PreventFatalDamage` marker (Death Ward), but that's an unconditional clamp; Undead Fortitude needs a save-gated variant with damage-type exclusions.
- **On-hit save-or-condition rider** (Ghoul's Paralyzing Touch; Dire Wolf's and Mastiff's bite knockdown; Gladiator Shield Bash STR save DC 15 vs prone for Medium-or-smaller targets) — claw or bite attack with a CON-vs-paralyzed or STR-vs-prone save on hit. The spell pipeline models this via the `conditionOnFail` save mechanic, but monsters can't yet wire a per-attack on-hit save without going through the spell planner.
- **On-hit auto-grapple** (Constrictor Snake's bite; Giant Frog's bite; Ankheg's bite, with restricted-target-of-bite-while-grappled rider; Tyrannosaurus Rex bite with restrained + restricted-target-of-bite rider on STR-save fail DC 17; Chain Devil chain on hit DC 14 escape) — bite attack with no save that automatically grapples / restrains the target on a hit (or on a save fail for T-Rex), with a fixed escape DC. Subset of the save-or-condition shape above without the save step (or one extra save step on T-Rex); the primitive needs to skip directly to a `ConditionApplied` on the target with the escape DC stamped onto the applied condition for future contested-check resolution. The grappled condition exists; the auto-apply pathway from a monster bite does not. The Ankheg / T-Rex variants add a "while a creature is grappled by the creature, it can only bite that creature and has advantage" rider, which is a self-targeting attack-restriction the engine doesn't model today.
- **Charge / Pounce / Trampling Charge** (Boar Charge; Minotaur Charge; Unicorn Charge; Allosaurus Pounce; Triceratops Trampling Charge; Mammoth Trampling Charge; Wereboar Tusk Charge) — movement-conditional damage rider: if the creature moves at least N ft in a straight line before the attack and hits, deal extra damage + STR save vs prone or push. Subset of the on-hit save-or-condition shape with an additional "moved at least N ft straight this turn" gate the engine doesn't track. Needs a movement-since-turn-start fact in the dispatcher. Allosaurus Pounce and the Trampling Charge variants both add a "if target is now prone, attacker can use bonus action for follow-up attack" rider, which composes cleanly with the existing bonus-action machinery once the prone-state check primitive lands.
- **Relentless** (Boar) — short-rest recharge ability: if reduced to 0 HP by damage of 7 or less, drops to 1 HP instead. Flat-damage-cap variant of Undead Fortitude (which uses a CON save). Same primitive family; could share an implementation once one lands.
- **Swallow** (Giant Frog) — compound multi-step action: bite that escalates to swallow on a Medium-or-smaller target, then deals recurring acid damage per turn while the target is inside, with the consumed creature gaining the blinded + restrained conditions and an escape mechanic that requires defeating the frog's HP threshold or a STR check. Composite of grapple + recurring damage + automatic-action-substitution while swallowed. No primitive composes for this today.
- **Degree-of-failure save tier** (Giant Centipede; Ghost's Horrifying Visage shares the shape with 1d4×10-year aging on fail-by-5) — save rider where failing by 5 or more compounds the effect. Needs a save-result tier primitive that branches on margin-of-failure, not just pass / fail. The existing `conditionOnFail` is binary.
- **Recharge-N actions** (Giant Spider's Web; Ghost's Possession recharge-6; future Young Red Dragon Breath Weapon, etc.) — actions usable once per encounter that recharge on a per-turn d6 roll. Needs both a monster-action vocabulary and a recharge primitive; neither exists.
- **Poison damage rider on attack** (Giant Spider, Giant Wolf Spider bite; Ankheg bite acid rider; Bone Devil sting poison rider; Assassin shortsword / light crossbow with DC 15 CON save vs 4d6 poison + poisoned 1 min half on save; Green Hag Claw 1d6 poison; Basilisk Bite 2d6 poison; Phase Spider Bite 2d8 poison; Drider Foreleg + Poison Burst; Medusa Snake Hair + Poison Ray) — extra damage of a specified type on a hit, with or without a save for half. Same shape gap as the on-hit-save-rider above, just with damage instead of a condition on the outcome. The Ankheg rider is unconditional extra damage on top of physical; the spider / Bone Devil / Assassin riders gate the damage on a CON save.
- **Blood Drain attach** (Stirge) — on a successful hit, the stirge attaches and auto-deals damage at the start of each of its turns until killed or removed. Needs an "attached" condition with recurring per-turn damage on a fixed target, plus an automatic action substitution while attached. No primitive composes for this today.
- **Web Sense / Web Walker** (Giant Spider, Giant Wolf Spider) — narrative; "ignore movement restrictions caused by webbing." No engine work needed (the engine doesn't model webbed terrain).
- **Max-HP-reduction-on-hit** (Specter's, Wraith's, Wight's Life Drain) — necrotic damage attack with a CON save; on failure, the target's maximum HP is reduced by the damage dealt until a long rest. Needs a separate hp-max-penalty primitive (the engine has `hp.maxBonus` for boosts like Aid, but no symmetric penalty channel).
- **Ability-score-reduction-on-hit** (Shadow's Strength Drain) — necrotic damage attack that also reduces the target's STR by 1d4; reaching STR 0 kills + spawns a new shadow. Needs an ability-score-modifier primitive applied as a per-hit rider plus the on-kill-spawn primitive below.
- **Incorporeal Movement** (Specter, Wraith, Ghost) — can move through other creatures and objects as if difficult terrain, takes 1d10 force damage if ending a turn inside an object. Needs a movement-mode + ending-position auto-damage primitive.
- **Environment-conditional advantage / disadvantage** (Specter's, Wraith's, Wight's Sunlight Sensitivity; Shadow's Sunlight Weakness; Shadow's Shadow Stealth bonus-action Hide in dim light / darkness; Stone Giant's Stone Camouflage advantage on Stealth in rocky terrain) — the advantage / disadvantage primitives exist (slice 97 closed the attack-side wiring); the gating fact "creature is in sunlight" or "in dim light" is consumer territory and needs a dispatcher fact for the engine to read.
- **On-kill-spawn** (Wraith's Create Specter; Shadow's STR-0 spawn) — when the rider's kill condition fires, a new creature appears under the killer's control. Needs a kill-trigger primitive composed with the existing summon system (slice 48).
- **Possession** (Ghost) — recharge-6 CHA save or the ghost replaces the target's body for up to a day (or until the body drops to 0 HP, the ghost is turned, etc). Needs a body-swap / control-takeover primitive. The "possessed" condition was previously flagged as missing from PfEoG's RAW deferral.
- **Etherealness** (Ghost) — action to enter / leave the Ethereal Plane. Cross-plane / phase primitive doesn't exist.
- **Amorphous / Standing Leap / Amphibious / Ethereal Sight / Spider Climb / False Appearance / Siege Monster / Ice Walk / Hold Breath** (Shadow, Giant Frog, Ghost; Black Pudding, Gelatinous Cube, Gray Ooze, Ochre Jelly Amorphous + Spider Climb; Gray Ooze False Appearance; Awakened Shrub / Awakened Tree / Treant False Appearance; Treant Siege Monster double-damage-to-objects; Young Black + Young Green + Adult Black + Adult Green Dragon Amphibious; Young White + Adult White Dragon Ice Walk free-movement-on-icy-difficult-terrain; Plesiosaurus Hold Breath 1 hour) — narrative movement / breathing / sensory / appearance traits. No engine work needed; consumer territory. The "blind beyond" qualifier on the oozes' / Shambling Mound's blindsight (creature is blind past the blindsight range) is similarly narrative under the current senses model. Siege Monster's "double damage to objects and structures" would only need a primitive once the engine targets non-creature objects.
- **Magic Resistance** (Imp, Quasit, Stone Golem, Iron Golem, Dryad, Satyr, Unicorn, Deva, Planetar, Solar, Bearded Devil, Barbed Devil, Chain Devil, Bone Devil; canonical across CR 5+ Fiends, Fey, Aberrations, Celestials, Dragons, Golems, and many higher-tier spellcasters) — advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. Two missing pieces: (a) `SetAdvantage.on` for saves today requires an ability key (`{kind: 'save', ability: 'STR'}` etc.), so there's no "all saves" variant to express "advantage on every save"; (b) there's no `event.isMagical` fact in the dispatcher to gate the advantage on a magical-source trigger. High-impact gap because Magic Resistance is shared across many MM creatures and will need to wire eventually.
- **Shapechanger** (Imp, Quasit; canonical to dozens of fey / fiend / monstrosity types and lycanthropes) — action to polymorph between fiend form and a beast form. The `polymorph` spell-planner exists (slice for polymorph spell), but monsters can't invoke it as a non-spell action; the planner expects a spell-cast entry point. Needs monster-action wiring composed with the polymorph planner.
- **Devil's Sight** (Imp, Lemure, Bearded Devil, Barbed Devil, Chain Devil, Bone Devil; canonical to nearly all devils and the warlock invocation of the same name) — magical darkness doesn't impede vision. Needs a vision-modifier primitive that interacts with the future-deferred "obscurement" / "darkness" primitives.
- **At-will Invisibility** (Imp, Quasit, Sprite) — action to turn invisible until the creature attacks, casts a spell, or concentration ends. The `invisible` condition exists; the at-will-action-to-self-apply pathway from a monster doesn't (closest existing analogue is the spell-side `engine.plan.invisibility`, but monsters can't invoke it).
- **Scare** (Quasit, 1/day) — bonus action, target within 20 ft, WIS save vs frightened 1 min with a recurring save at end of each of target's turns. Same shape as Hold Person's `recurringSave.onSuccess: 'removeCondition'`, but applied via a monster bonus action.
- **Fetid Cloud** (Dretch, 1/day) — 10-ft radius poisonous cloud, CON save vs poisoned 1 min, recurring save at end of turn. Same shape as the area-effect spell mechanic already partially shipped (slices 68-72); could share once monsters can invoke area-effect actions.
- **Hellish Rejuvenation** (Lemure) — when killed outside Avernus, the lemure reforms in Avernus after some time. Narrative; consumer territory.
- **Rock thrown attack** (all six Giants) — ranged-weapon attack with a fixed rock damage die scaled by the giant's tier. Subset of the monster-action vocabulary gap (same shape as the bite / claw / weapon attacks across the rest of the bestiary); the rocks themselves aren't modeled as item-instances because monster statblocks have no `actions` field linking them.
- **Rock Catching reaction** (Stone Giant) — reaction to catch or reduce damage from a thrown rock, sling stone, or arrow if the giant succeeds on a DC 10 Dex save. Same gap shape as Bandit Captain's / Knight's Parry: there's no generic monster-reaction primitive.
- **Lightning Strike** (Storm Giant) — recharge-5-or-6 action: ranged lightning bolt targeting up to two creatures within 500 ft, DC 17 DEX save for half. Needs both the recharge primitive (same family as Giant Spider's Web and Ghost's Possession) and the monster-action vocabulary; the multi-target ranged-save shape itself is well-trodden via the spell pipeline.
- **Innate Spellcasting (per-spell envelope flavor)** (Cloud Giant: Detect Magic / Fog Cloud at-will, Feather Fall / Fly / Misty Step / Telekinesis 3/day, Control Weather / Gaseous Form 1/day; Storm Giant: Detect Magic / Feather Fall / Levitate / Light at-will, Control Weather / Water Breathing 3/day; Dryad: Druidcraft at-will, Entangle / Goodberry 3/day, Barkskin / Pass without Trace / Shillelagh 1/day; Couatl: Detect Evil and Good / Detect Magic / Detect Thoughts at-will, Bless / Create Food and Water / Cure Wounds / Lesser Restoration / Protection from Poison / Sanctuary / Shield 3/day, Dream / Greater Restoration / Scrying 1/day; Unicorn: Detect Evil and Good / Druidcraft / Pass without Trace at-will, Calm Emotions / Dispel Evil and Good / Entangle 1/day; Deva: Detect Evil and Good at-will, Commune / Raise Dead 1/day; Planetar: Detect Evil and Good / Invisibility self-only at-will, Blade Barrier / Dispel Evil and Good / Flame Strike / Raise Dead 3/day, Commune / Control Weather / Insect Plague 1/day; Solar: Detect Evil and Good / Invisibility self-only at-will, Blade Barrier / Dispel Evil and Good / Resurrection 3/day, Commune / Control Weather 1/day) — same monster-spellcasting deferral as the wizard / priest / druid NPCs, but with the per-spell at-will / per-day usage envelope rather than a slot table. Both shapes need to wire together; per-spell envelopes are the simpler half and can ship first. Couatl's 13-entry at-will + 3/day + 1/day list is the densest single-monster spell list in the pack and the canonical proof that the envelope shape needs to scale to long lists per statblock.
- **Elemental Form movement modes** (Air Elemental's Air Form; Water Elemental's Water Form; Fire Elemental's Fire Form; Earth Elemental's Earth Glide) — narrative movement abilities (enter a creature's space, move through 1-inch openings, burrow through nonmagical earth without disturbing surface, etc.) plus passing-through-creature interactions. Largely consumer territory (the engine doesn't model positions or terrain), but the Earth Glide / Air Form interaction with grapple-style positional rules would need a movement-mode primitive if positional rules ever land.
- **Damage-on-contact + on-grapple** (Fire Elemental's Fire Form: any creature touching the elemental or hit by its melee attack takes fire damage; any creature grappled by the elemental takes recurring fire damage at the start of each of the elemental's turns) — needs an on-grappled-by-self recurring damage rider plus a touch-trigger damage primitive. Composes with the existing condition framework once monster bonus actions / reactions wire in.
- **Environmental damage susceptibility** (Fire Elemental's Water Susceptibility: 1 cold damage per 5 ft moved in water / per gallon splashed) — narrative / environmental, consumer territory. The damage type is mechanical but the trigger (environmental contact with water) isn't an engine fact today.
- **Freeze on cold damage** (Water Elemental: when it takes cold damage, partially freezes; speed becomes 0 until end of next turn) — needs an on-damage-typed reaction rider that applies a temporary movement modifier. Similar shape to other on-damage-typed triggers; the speed-set-to-zero is a clean modifier but the trigger gate (specifically cold damage taken) needs an `event.damageType` fact at the dispatcher.
- **Permanent magical Invisibility** (Invisible Stalker) — always invisible except when killed or banished; can be perceived only by truesight or by an active Faerie Fire / See Invisibility effect. The `invisible` condition exists; the always-on-from-spawn application (rather than action-triggered) is a different installation path than at-will Invisibility (Imp / Quasit gap). Both routes need a generic monster-condition-from-statblock pathway, currently absent.
- **Faultless Tracker** (Invisible Stalker) — when summoned, the stalker knows the location of its quarry as long as both are on the same plane; tracks even through illusion or polymorph. Narrative; consumer territory until the engine models locations / planes.
- **Deadly Leap** (Bulette) — bonus-action movement that lands in a target's space and forces a STR-or-DEX save (DC 16) on every Large-or-smaller creature in the landing area: on failure the target takes B+S damage and is knocked prone, on success it takes half damage and moves 5 ft to the nearest unoccupied space. Multi-target save shape from the spell pipeline plus a bonus-action move primitive plus the position-resolution step (target-takes-prone, save-success-moves-aside) the engine doesn't model.
- **~~Recharge area-save Breath Weapons~~** ✓ shipped (slice 140). New `BreathWeaponSpec` field on `MonsterStatblock` + `breathWeaponExpended` runtime flag on `Character` + `BreathWeaponFired` / `BreathWeaponRecharged` events + `planBreathWeapon` (fires action + per-target save + halve-on-success damage) + `planBreathWeaponRechargeAtTurnStart` hooked into `planAdvanceTurn` / `planBeginFirstTurn`. 12 statblocks wired in slice 140 alongside the primitive: Young Red Dragon (fire breath 30 ft cone DC 17 16d6), Young White / Black / Green / Blue Dragons (cone / line shapes at rechargeMin 5 with their canonical elemental types and dice), Adult Red / White / Black / Green / Blue Dragons (60-90 ft cones / lines, 14d8-22d6 damage at rechargeMin 5), Iron Golem (15 ft cone, 10d8 poison at rechargeMin 6), Ankheg (30 ft line, 3d6 acid at rechargeMin 6). Save passes `sourceIsMagical: true` so Magic Resistance is honored. Cone / line area-of-effect target inclusion stays consumer territory (the engine doesn't model LOS or positional area resolution); consumers / DMs supply the affected target list per the area shape on the statblock. The narrower "recharge-N" bullet above this one covers single-target recharge actions (Giant Spider Web, Ghost Possession, etc.) which still need the same primitive's recharge half plus a single-target rider; the breath-weapon-specific shape lands at slice 140 and the single-target variant is a small follow-up.
- **Tail Spike ammunition** (Manticore: 24 spikes, expended on attack and regrow on long rest) — depletable per-creature ammunition tied to a specific attack, refreshed by long rest. The pack's depleting-resource shapes are spell slots, action surges, etc., all character-side; monsters can't yet carry per-attack ammo pools without an ammunition primitive on the statblock.
- **Variable AC by posture** (Ankheg: AC 14 normally, AC 11 while prone) — the statblock schema carries a single `ac: number`, so prone-AC, helpless-AC, etc. variants are dropped today. Common across the bestiary; tracked as a narrative deferral for now since RAW prone already grants attack advantage and most consumers don't surface posture-specific AC.
- **Split on damage-type** (Black Pudding, Ochre Jelly) — when a Medium-or-larger ooze with at least 10 HP takes lightning or slashing damage, it splits into two new oozes, each with HP equal to half the original (rounded down). New mechanic kin to the existing on-kill-spawn deferral (Wraith Create Specter, Shadow STR-0 spawn), but the trigger is "damage of a specific type received" rather than reduction to 0 HP, and the new instances aren't allies — they're independent copies of the original statblock. Two additional twists: the oozes that split are also immune to the very damage types that trigger the split (lightning and slashing), so the trigger fires regardless of how much damage actually landed. Needs `event.damageType` plus a "spawn-copy-of-self" primitive composed with the existing summon system (slice 48).
- **Engulf** (Gelatinous Cube; Shambling Mound's Engulf-after-double-grapple-hit variant) — composite movement action: the cube moves up to its speed, entering Large-or-smaller creature spaces; engulfed creatures DEX save DC 12 vs. taking acid damage and being restrained inside, with recurring acid damage and air-loss tracking each turn until escape (STR or DEX check). Composes movement + multi-target save + recurring per-turn damage + escape-via-check on a restrained variant. Similar shape to Giant Frog's Swallow (already deferred) but operates on creature spaces during movement rather than as a swallow-after-bite chain. The Shambling Mound variant is triggered by a two-slam-hit chain rather than by movement: if both slam attacks hit a Medium-or-smaller target in the same turn, the target is grappled (DC 14 escape) and the mound automatically Engulfs them with recurring 2d8+4 bludgeoning while smothered.
- **Transparent** (Gelatinous Cube) — even with darkvision a creature must succeed on a DC 15 Perception check to detect the cube. Partial overlap with the keen-senses-family gap (the engine doesn't model perception-check DCs against specific creatures today); fully addressing it needs a "creature is hidden / disguised / transparent" predicate fact in the dispatcher plus a per-creature observer-Perception check on first encounter.
- **Corrosive Form on melee hit** (Black Pudding) — creatures that hit the pudding with a melee attack take 4 acid damage automatically (no save, no attack roll). Inverse of the on-hit damage rider above: the pudding is the *target* of the trigger and the attacker takes splash damage. Needs an attack-against-self trigger ("creature melee-hits me") plus a damage-back primitive. Composable with the existing reaction architecture once monster reactions land.
- **Item degradation on hit** (Black Pudding, Gray Ooze: nonmagical metal / wood weapons that hit corrode cumulatively, destroyed at -5; nonmagical armor partially dissolved by the pudding's pseudopod) — weapon and armor degradation isn't modeled by the engine today (no item-instance durability counter). Consumer territory until item-instance hit-points / wear primitives exist.
- **Antimagic Susceptibility** (Animated Armor, Flying Sword, Rug of Smothering) — the construct is incapacitated if hit by *dispel magic*; if targeted by it, the construct's creator (or itself) must succeed on a CON save vs the caster's DC or fall unconscious for 1 minute. Specific-spell-as-trigger primitive: needs `event.spellId === 'dispel-magic'` dispatcher fact plus a save-or-condition rider scoped to the named spell. Closest existing analogue is the slice-50 family of trigger-on-event-kind riders, but the trigger key today is the event kind, not a spell id.
- **Damage Transfer while grappling** (Rug of Smothering) — while the rug grapples a creature, it takes only half the damage dealt to it; the other half is dealt to the grappled creature. Composite of damage-redirect (half to self, half to grapple-target) gated on the grapple-state condition. The redirect primitive doesn't exist; cleanest install would be a damage-event rewrite step that fires when the receiving creature has a `Grappling(target)` state.
- **Smother / suffocation rider** (Rug of Smothering) — Smother attack auto-grapples a Medium-or-smaller target and applies restrained + blinded + start-of-each-turn 2d6+3 bludgeoning damage; while smothered, the target is at risk of suffocation. Composite of auto-grapple + multi-condition application + recurring damage + suffocation. The suffocation rules (RAW: 1 + CON-mod minutes of held breath, then 1d6 dropping to 0) aren't currently modeled.
- **Bound amulet** (Shield Guardian) — the guardian is magically bound to an amulet; the amulet's wearer can use a bonus action to call or dismiss the guardian, and the guardian appears within 30 ft of the wearer. Cross-entity binding plus consumer-territory teleportation. Needs an inter-instance "owner" reference, which neither the statblock schema nor the item-instance schema currently models.
- **Regeneration on partial HP** (Shield Guardian: regains 10 HP at start of its turn if HP > 0) — unconditional start-of-turn HP regen on a monster, gated on "not at 0 HP". The existing trigger-on-turn-start machinery exists for riders (Stirge Blood Drain, Specter Life Drain shape), but unconditional HP regain on the bearer of the trait is a new shape. Composable with the existing recurring-rider system once monster turn-start triggers can apply self-targeted Heal effects.
- **Spell Storing** (Shield Guardian) — a spellcaster wearing the amulet can use an action to store a spell of 4th level or lower in the guardian; the spell is released by command or as a reaction. Composite of a "capture next casted spell as data" primitive + a deferred-cast pathway + a reaction-trigger to release. Spell-as-data carrier doesn't exist today.
- **Damage redirect to self reaction** (Shield Guardian's Shield: when the wearer of its amulet takes damage and is within 60 ft, the guardian takes the damage instead) — Inverse of the Damage Transfer rider above. Damage-event interception that re-targets the damage onto a different creature. Same primitive family.
- **Immutable Form** (Stone Golem, Iron Golem) — immune to any spell or effect that would alter the creature's form. Needs an "immune to specific spell/effect kind" predicate; this is sharper than the existing condition-immunity surface because the trigger is a spell-or-effect category (polymorph, alter self, etc.), not a condition. Same primitive shape as Antimagic Susceptibility above but as immunity rather than a trigger.
- **Magic Weapons** (Stone Golem, Iron Golem) — the creature's weapon attacks are magical for resistance / immunity purposes. The inverse of `GrantResistance.qualifier: 'magical'` on the receiving side; the engine fact `event.isMagical` (queued for Magic Resistance) is the same fact needed here, just consulted by the attacker's resistance/immunity check. Lands for free with that slice.
- **Slow Recharge** (Stone Golem, recharge 5-6) — targets all creatures within 10 ft; WIS save DC 17 or affected by *slow* spell for 1 minute. Compose recharge-N + multi-target save + spell-as-monster-action (apply the named spell's effect rather than carry its planner). Spell-as-effect-installer pathway doesn't exist today; closest is the spell pipeline itself, which assumes a caster.
- **Fire Absorption / Lightning Absorption (damage-as-heal by type)** (Iron Golem Fire Absorption; Shambling Mound Lightning Absorption) — when subjected to damage of the specified type, the creature takes no damage and instead regains HP equal to what would have been dealt. Composite of damage-immunity (already supported via `damageImmunities`) + a heal-from-damage primitive. The damage-immunity half is in the statblock; the heal-on-damage-type half needs `event.damageType` (queued from Water Elemental's Freeze gap) plus a damage-as-heal primitive. Same primitive family for both, parameterized by damage type.
- **Adamantine-qualified resistance** (Stone Golem, Iron Golem: B/P/S resistance "from nonmagical attacks that aren't adamantine") — same gap shape as Wight / Imp / Lemure's silvered qualifier in batches 1.6 and 1.7. The slice-112 `GrantResistance.qualifier` enum currently models only `'nonmagical' | 'magical'`; adamantine, silvered, and cold-iron weapon-material qualifiers are RAW-pervasive across constructs / undead / fiends / fey but currently overstate to "all nonmagical".
- **Comprehended-but-not-spoken / creator-derived languages** (Shield Guardian: understands commands in any language but doesn't speak; Stone Golem, Iron Golem: understand creator's languages but don't speak; Pegasus: understands Celestial, Common, Elvish, Sylvan but doesn't speak; Awakened Shrub, Awakened Tree: one language known to its creator) — the schema's `languages: string[]` is bidirectional today; it doesn't distinguish "can comprehend" from "can speak", nor does it carry a "this creature inherits a language from another entity at instantiation" link. A small primitive split (`comprehendedLanguages` / `spokenLanguages` + an optional `inheritsLanguagesFrom` reference) would close both shapes; currently dropped as narrative for these seven entries.
- **Telepathy as a sense** (Aboleth 120 ft, Otyugh limited 120 ft) — the schema today carries telepathy as a free-text entry in the `languages` array (e.g. `"telepathy 120 ft"`). RAW telepathy is a directional communication channel with a range, not a language; modeling it cleanly would split `languages` from `telepathyRange` on the statblock. Otyugh's "limited telepathy" (short messages only) is a further qualifier worth representing as a flag once the split exists.
- **Legendary Actions** (Aboleth, Adult White Dragon, Adult Black Dragon, Adult Green Dragon, Adult Blue Dragon, Adult Red Dragon; canonical across most CR 10+ MM solo monsters) — wholly-new mechanic family. A creature with legendary actions can take a fixed number (typically 3) of special actions at the end of each other creature's turn, drawn from a menu specific to the statblock. Needs (a) a `legendaryActions` field on `MonsterStatblock` carrying a per-statblock menu, (b) a per-turn budget tracker on the runtime creature instance, and (c) an end-of-other-creature's-turn trigger in the turn-cycle. Composes with the existing trigger dispatch architecture but adds a new turn-cycle phase. The Adult Chromatic Dragons share an identical legendary-action menu (Detect, Tail Attack, Wing Attack costs 2); once the shape lands, all five wire as data with parameterized DCs.
- **Legendary Resistance** (Aboleth 3/day; Adult White, Adult Black, Adult Green, Adult Blue, Adult Red Dragons 3/day each; Adult Brass, Adult Copper, Adult Bronze, Adult Silver, Adult Gold Dragons 3/day each; Balor 3/day; Vampire 3/day; Mummy Lord 3/day; Pit Fiend 4/day; Ancient White, Ancient Black, Ancient Green, Ancient Blue, Ancient Red Dragons 4/day each; Ancient Brass, Ancient Copper, Ancient Bronze, Ancient Silver, Ancient Gold Dragons 4/day each; canonical across CR 13+ solo monsters) — when the creature fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead. Per-day budget primitive similar to per-day spell envelopes; the install point is a save-result rewrite that fires after a failed save and consumes a charge. Composes with the existing save resolution pipeline.
- **Enslave (recurring save vs charmed)** (Aboleth, 3/day: target within 30 ft, WIS save DC 14 vs charmed by aboleth indefinitely; repeats save once per day, ends at distance 1+ mile or aboleth dies, gives telepathic command channel) — per-day usage envelope + WIS-save-vs-charmed-condition + day-cadence recurring save + ability-to-command-via-telepathy + range-based-condition-end. Closest existing analogue is the recurring-save family (Hold Person, Quasit Scare) but extends with the per-day-cadence recurring save (not per-turn) and a "condition ends when distance from source exceeds X" expiry predicate.
- **Disease on hit** (Aboleth tentacle: target diseased, can only breathe underwater, can't regain HP except by magic; Otyugh bite: DC 15 CON save vs disease, similar effect) — same shape as the on-hit save-or-condition rider, but the resulting condition is `diseased` with the specific RAW effects baked in. The `diseased` condition itself doesn't exist in the pack's conditions list today; would need both a generic disease condition with parameterized effects and the per-disease effect carrier (Aboleth's specific disease vs. Otyugh's).
- **Mucous Cloud** (Aboleth, while underwater) — creature touching the aboleth or melee-hitting it within 5 ft must CON save DC 14 vs the aboleth-tentacle disease. Compose damage-on-melee-hit reaction shape (Black Pudding's Corrosive Form gap) + save-or-disease rider + environmental gating (must be underwater).
- **Probing Telepathy** (Aboleth) — when a creature communicates telepathically with the aboleth, the aboleth learns the creature's greatest desire if it can see the creature. Pure narrative reveal mechanic; no engine work needed.
- **Aberrant Ground** (Gibbering Mouther) — ground within 10 ft of the mouther is difficult terrain. Area-effect terrain modifier; the engine doesn't model positions or terrain, so consumer territory.
- **Gibbering** (Gibbering Mouther: creatures within 20 ft that can hear must succeed on DC 10 WIS save at start of their turn or roll d8 to determine actions for that turn) — recurring per-turn-start save vs a random-action chaos mechanic. Composite: per-turn-start trigger on every nearby creature + save vs effect + random-action picker. The chaos mechanic itself (8-way action substitution at the start of an affected creature's turn) is a novel primitive.
- **Blinding Spittle Recharge** (Gibbering Mouther, recharge 5-6: ranged 15-ft point, DEX save DC 10 vs blinded until end of mouther's next turn) — same recharge + area-save shape as Acid Spray / Poison Breath, but with a blinded-condition rider rather than damage. Folds into the same composite once monster recharge + condition-on-fail composes.
- **Tentacle Slam multi-target save** (Otyugh, while grappling two creatures: each grappled target STR save DC 14 vs 2d6 bludgeoning + stunned 1 round; half damage and no stun on success) — multi-target save action with a "must currently be grappling" precondition. Composes save-or-condition + multi-target-save shapes already deferred; the precondition is the only new bit.
- **Bonus-action teleport (non-spell)** (Blink Dog Blink: 40 ft teleport to a visible unoccupied space) — the engine has the spell-side `engine.plan.misty-step` for teleport-as-spell, but monsters can't invoke it as a non-spell action and the planner expects a spell-cast entry point. Needs a monster-bonus-action vocabulary entry composed with a generic teleport primitive shared between misty-step / dimension door / blink / monster-flavor variants.
- **Fey Charm** (Dryad, action: target one humanoid or beast within 30 ft, WIS save DC 14 vs charmed for 24 hours; charmed target regards dryad as trusted friend; only one creature charmed at a time, ending an existing charm refreshes the slot) — same recurring-save-vs-charmed family as Aboleth's Enslave (batch 1.13) and Quasit's Scare (batch 1.7), but with three new wrinkles: a flat 24-hour duration rather than indefinite, the one-active-target slot (incompatible with the existing per-action-cast model), and ending the charm by inflicting damage from the dryad on the charmed target (so the charm-condition install needs an "ends when caster damages target" expiry predicate). Same primitive family as Enslave with these additional expiry shapes.
- **Speak with Beasts and Plants / Tree Stride** (Dryad) — narrative communication and consumer-territory teleport-via-tree-network respectively. No engine work needed for either today.
- **Mirthful Leaps / Reveler / Standing Leap variants** (Satyr Mirthful Leaps: long jump 30 ft, high jump 15 ft; Satyr Reveler: advantage on Persuasion / Performance with celebrating creatures; same family as Bulette Standing Leap and the existing narrative-movement deferral) — all narrative; consumer territory.
- **Heart Sight** (Sprite: action within 5 ft, CHA save DC 10 vs sprite learning target's emotional state + best / worst moments of last 24 hours) — narrative reveal mechanic; consumer territory until the engine grows a player-facing memory model.
- **Sleep-poison bow** (Sprite shortbow: 1 damage + CON save DC 10 vs poisoned 1 min; if target falls to 0 HP from sleep effect, unconscious for 1 min, wakes on damage or 1-action shake) — composite of on-hit save-vs-poisoned + a sleep-substitution-of-unconsciousness-at-zero-HP rider. The sleep-substitution shape is the new bit (closest analogue is *sleep* spell mechanics, which the pack already wires).
- **Magic Weapons (Construct + Celestial variants)** (Stone Golem, Iron Golem, Couatl, Unicorn) — the creature's weapon attacks are magical for resistance / immunity purposes. Same primitive family as the Magic Weapons gap already filed for the Golems; folding the Celestials in here. Lands together with the `event.isMagical` dispatcher fact.
- **Unconditional elemental rider on bite** (Deva +4d8 radiant, Planetar +5d8 radiant, Solar +6d8 radiant; Young White Dragon bite +1d8 cold, Young Black Dragon bite +1d8 acid, Young Green Dragon bite +1d6 poison, Young Blue Dragon bite +1d10 lightning; Adult White Dragon bite +1d8 cold, Adult Black Dragon bite +1d8 acid, Adult Green Dragon bite +2d6 poison, Adult Blue Dragon bite +1d10 lightning, Adult Red Dragon bite +4d6 fire) — every melee attack the creature makes deals extra typed damage automatically (no save, the rider is baked into the attack itself). Composite of Magic Weapons + flat-extra-damage-on-attack rider; the rider here is unconditional (no save, no extra roll) which is simpler than the spider / ankheg variants of the damage-rider-on-attack gap. Could share a primitive with the Sneak Attack / Smite per-hit damage-add machinery. Same primitive family, different scope predicate.
- **Healing Touch (per-day-budget action)** (Unicorn 3/day, Deva 3/day, Planetar 4/day, Solar 4/day; restore HP + remove curse / disease / poison conditions on touch) — same per-day usage envelope as innate spellcasting (already deferred), but the action is a heal rather than a spell. The heal-on-touch shape doesn't need a spell-cast entry today; could ship as a generic monster-action variant once monster actions wire.
- **Change Shape (monster polymorph action)** (Deva 1/day; polymorph into a humanoid or beast of CR equal or lower than the deva, or back to true form) — same composite as the existing Shapechanger deferral (Imp / Quasit polymorph between fiend and beast forms in batch 1.7) but with broader form-target rules (any humanoid OR beast at CR-cap) and a per-day usage envelope. Folds into the same monster-polymorph pathway gap.

- **Shielded Mind** (Couatl; immune to scrying, emotion-sensing, mind-reading, and divination location-sensing) — composite immunity that spans several spell schools / effect categories. Lands once that predicate slot exists.
- **Slaying Longbow** (Solar; ranged attack deals piercing + 6d8 radiant + DC 15 CON save or extra 24 (6d8+4) necrotic; if target dies from this attack, the soul departs) — composite of attack-deals-typed-damage + on-hit save-vs-extra-damage + an "if death, soul-departs" instant-kill flag. The save-vs-extra-damage shape is novel (closest is the damage-on-fail mechanic used by spells like Poison Spray, but applied as a weapon rider rather than spell effect). The soul-departure flag is narrative.
- **Flying Sword (animated-weapon bonus action)** (Solar; bonus action releases the longsword to float and attack autonomously for 1 minute) — creates a transient autonomous attacker controlled by the original creature. - **Frightful Presence (aura WIS save vs frightened)** (Adult White DC 14, Adult Black DC 16, Adult Green DC 16, Adult Blue DC 17, Adult Red DC 19; Chain Devil Unnerving Mask DC 14 single-target action variant with appearance-of-deceased-loved-one flavor; canonical across Adult+ dragons, many demon lords, archfey, and other CR 13+ apex monsters) — when a creature starts its turn within 120 ft of the dragon and can see it, the creature must WIS save vs. frightened condition for 1 minute; a successful save grants immunity to that dragon's Frightful Presence for 24 hours; frightened target repeats save at end of each of its turns. - **Stomp (prone-target-only attack)** (Triceratops Stomp 3d10+6 bludgeoning vs prone targets only; Mammoth Stomp 4d10+7 bludgeoning vs prone targets only) — a monster action whose use is gated on target state (target must be prone). Three cross-cutting structural gaps surface across all 111 entries and aren't specific to any single creature:

- **~~Monster engine-data not consumed~~** ✓ shipped (slices 129 + 130 + 131). Slice 129 wired the resistance / immunity / vulnerability / condition-immunity arrays plus the `traits` Effect array via a new `collectMonsterEffects` helper in `buildEffectStack` (synthesized `GrantResistance` / `GrantImmunity` / `GrantVulnerability` / `GrantConditionImmunity` entries from the four scalar arrays, appends `traits[]` verbatim, `mitigateDamage` and `isImmuneToCondition` consume the accumulator unchanged). Slice 130 wired `savingThrows: Record<string, number>` via a fast-path in `computeSavingThrow`: when the character resolves to a content-pack monster with a listed save bonus, the baked RAW total (already includes ability mod + proficiency per MM presentation) replaces the ability-mod + class-proficiency reconstruction. Slice 131 wired the Magic Resistance primitive: new `GrantMagicResistance` marker Effect, accumulator's `hasMagicResistance()` query, and a `sourceIsMagical?: boolean` parameter on `ComputeSaveInput` that contributes advantage when both flags are set. `planCastSpell`'s save mechanic passes `sourceIsMagical: true` and now honors `hasAdvantage` / `hasDisadvantage` by rolling 2d20 take-max / take-min (pre-slice 131 it ignored save advantage entirely). Effects / exhaustion / advantage still layer on top. Skeleton's bludgeoning vulnerability, Young Red Dragon's fire immunity + listed saves, every Undead and Fiend's nonmagical B/P/S resistance, every conditionImmunity entry, the caster-NPC saves, and Imp + Quasit's Magic Resistance all light up at runtime. Slice 133 extended `sourceIsMagical: true` to trap (Glyph of Warding, Cordon of Arrows), recurring-save (Hold Person and friends), and Sanctuary's ward-bypass save; trap and recurring-save also gained 2d20-take-max / take-min support. Stunning Strike stays non-magical (Monk Discipline is not a spell per RAW). Magic Resistance now propagates across every magical save path in the engine.
- **No actions field on `MonsterStatblock`** — the schema carries `traits: EffectSchema[]` but no `actions`; bite / slam / claw / weapon attacks on these statblocks are narrative only. Several weapon items exist in the pack with monster-named ids (`dragon-bite`, `dragon-claw`, `ogre-greatclub`) but nothing links the statblocks to those items, so they're orphaned today.
- **No `creatureSubtype` field beyond the existing `subtype: string`** — fine for most cases, but Goblinoid / Devil / Demon etc. tag-style subtypes (used elsewhere in the rules for targeting / immunity gates) would benefit from being a constrained enum.
