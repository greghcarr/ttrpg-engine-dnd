Cohort highlights: (1) Swarm of Crawling Claws ships the largest condition-immunity profile in the pack (11 entries: charmed + exhaustion + frightened + grappled + incapacitated + paralyzed + petrified + poisoned + prone + restrained + stunned — the canonical "swarm-of-Tiny" immunity bundle reflecting the non-individual-creature nature).
