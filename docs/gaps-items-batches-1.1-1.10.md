### Deferred mechanical wiring (current pack)

Every magic item except Wand of Magic Missiles ships with `effects: []` today. Each entry below names the engine primitive that would walk the item from schema-only to wired. Items grouped by the primitive they share.

- **Conditional advantage / disadvantage grant** (no primitive yet): Cloak of Elvenkind (advantage on Stealth checks vs creatures relying on sight; disadvantage on enemy Perception checks to spot the wearer), Eyes of the Eagle (advantage on sight-based Perception). Needs a per-skill-roll context that can swing a check from another creature's perspective; the existing `SetAdvantage` shape is bearer-side only.
- **~~Passive sense grant~~** ✓ shipped (slices 127 + 132). Goggles of Night now wires `{ kind: 'GrantSense', sense: 'darkvision', range: 60 }` and the slice-132 projection pass folds it into the wearer's effect stack from inventory (no attunement required per RAW).
- **~~Passive movement-mode grant~~** ✓ shipped (slices 132 + 139). Slice 132 wired Ring of Swimming (`ModifySpeed swim set 40` from inventory, no attunement); slice 139 wired Slippers of Spider Climbing (`ModifySpeed climb set 30` via the attuned path), Cloak of the Manta Ray (`ModifySpeed swim set 60`, no attunement; underwater-breathing arm deferred behind the breathlessness-marker primitive), and Gloves of Swimming and Climbing (`ModifySpeed climb set 30` + `ModifySpeed swim set 30` + `SetAdvantage on skill:athletics`).
- **~~Resistance + targeted spell-immunity~~** ✓ shipped for the force-resistance half (slice 139): Brooch of Shielding now wires `{ kind: 'GrantResistance', damageType: 'force' }`. The magic-missile-specific immunity arm still needs a "spell-id immunity" gate the engine doesn't carry yet (closest neighbour is `GrantConditionImmunity`).
- **Self-stabilization + healing maximization**: Periapt of Wound Closure (auto-stable at 0 HP; max die on Hit Die spend). The auto-stable arm needs a death-save replacement primitive (closely related to Death Ward but applies at 0 HP, not on the killing blow). The max-die arm needs a Hit Die spend resolver to consult a "maximize" flag.
- **At-will spell-from-item**: Hat of Disguise (cast Disguise Self at will, no slot or components), Helm of Telepathy (cast Detect Thoughts and Suggestion via the helm's per-day budget), Decanter of Endless Water (command-word water creation). Needs an `ItemSpellGrant { spellId, atWill?: boolean, chargesPerUse?: number }` effect kind plus a planner that consumes item charges (or skips when `atWill`) instead of the wearer's spell slots. Helm of Telepathy's per-day budget specifically needs the charges shape attached to the spell grant; Decanter's three command words (Stream / Fountain / Geyser) are pure narrative until either an environment-effects primitive or a fixed-effect catalog lands.
- **Ability-score override** (no primitive yet): Headband of Intellect (INT becomes 19), Belt of Hill Giant Strength (STR becomes 21). Needs an `OverrideAbilityScore { ability: AbilityScore, value: number }` effect kind plus an `acquireAbilityScore` derive helper that honors the max-of-override-and-base RAW semantic (the item doesn't lower a higher base). Belt-of-Giant-Strength's family (hill / stone / frost / fire / cloud / storm) will share the same primitive at different `value` targets when authored together.
- **Composite movement + jump multiplier**: Boots of Striding and Springing (walking speed becomes 30 if lower; long-jump and high-jump distances tripled; no running start needed). The speed-floor arm needs a `ModifySpeed` extension with an `op: 'floor'` variant (existing ops are `add` / `multiply` / `set`); the jump-multiplier arm needs a jump-distance derive surface the engine doesn't currently model. The "no running start" arm is consumer-side narrative.
- **Flight while attuned with conditional trigger**: Cloak of the Bat (advantage on Stealth + fly speed 40 ft in dim light or darkness). The flight arm composes the existing `ModifySpeed { mode: 'fly' }` primitive with the conditional-buff-by-environment shape the engine doesn't carry (lighting conditions aren't a tracked fact). The Stealth-advantage arm needs the same conditional-bearer-side advantage primitive Cloak of Elvenkind is waiting on.
- **Environmental immunity + alternate respiration**: Necklace of Adaptation (no need to breathe; immunity to harmful gases). Needs a `GrantBreathlessness` marker effect plus an environment-damage-source filter (the engine has `GrantDamageImmunity` per damage type but no "harmful gas" category, which RAW spans poison + acid + custom-DM environment damage).
- **Paired-item utility / sender-receiver linkage**: Sending Stones (cast Sending between paired stones, 1/day). Same `ItemSpellGrant` primitive as Hat of Disguise, plus a paired-instance linkage that the existing item-instance schema doesn't model (each stone needs a `pairedItemInstanceId` pointer to scope the Sending spell's target to the partner stone). Without the linkage the spell-grant fires correctly but loses the RAW partner-only constraint.
- **~~Consume-side effect resolver~~** ✓ shipped (slices 235 / 236 / 237) + sweep (slice 239). The `ConsumeItem` planner + `ConsumeAction` discriminated union (Heal / ApplyCondition / CastSpell) cover the bulk of the SRD potion catalog. Slice 235 wired the 4 healing potions; slice 236 wired Potion of Climbing + Potion of Water Breathing; slice 237 wired 6 spell scrolls; slice 239 swept 14 more consumables onto the existing 3 variants (Potion of Growth / Diminution / Invisibility / Flying / Speed / Animal Friendship / Mind Reading / 6 Giant Strength + Oil of Slipperiness). 20+ consumables now wired. - **Items-batch-1.9 specific named weapons** (extend beyond batch 1.5's generic +N templates with the iconic DMG weapon roster). All eight ship as `itemKind: 'magic'` with `effects: []` and no charges, matching the existing Flametongue Longsword shape rather than the base-weapon shape (consumer-side instantiation overlays the magic-item template onto a base weapon, same as Weapon +1 / +2 / +3). The cross-cutting blocker is still the magic-item-effect-projection pass already named for ~20 wondrous items, plus the permanent-attack-and-damage-bonus extension of the slice-76 `ItemInstance.temporaryBuff` shape that batch 1.5 named. The new per-item shapes this batch introduces:
  - *Vicious Weapon* and *Vorpal Sword*: on-crit special effect. Vicious adds extra 7 damage on a crit; Vorpal severs the target's head on a natural 20 (instant kill on a head-bearing slashing-vulnerable creature). The `planAttack` crit-resolution chain doesn't currently have an item-driven extension point for "additional damage on crit" or "alternate outcome on natural 20"; needs a new `WeaponCritRider { extraDice?, alternateOutcome? }` hook the planner consults when an attack's crit-confirmation arm fires. The Vorpal "head and not immune to slashing" gate is a target-predicate the engine can express today via creature traits.
  - *Sun Blade* and *Holy Avenger*: type-conditional damage rider. Sun Blade adds +2 damage vs Undead and deals radiant damage as its base type; Holy Avenger (paladin-attunement) adds +1d10 radiant vs Fiends and Undead. Composes with the existing damage-rider primitives (`AddModifier { target: 'damage', value: N }` and `AddDamageDice { dice, damageType, when }`) once the conditional-by-target-type-tag fact is available; the engine already tags creatures by type in monster statblocks, so `event.targetType` would be the natural fact addition.
  - *Frost Brand*: composite passive. Extra 1d6 cold damage on hit (same `AddDamageDice` shape Sun Blade and Holy Avenger want); fire resistance while held (same `GrantResistance { damageType: 'fire' }` projection that the magic-item-effect-projection pass enables); extinguishes nonmagical fires within 30 ft (pure narrative). All three arms wire once projection lands except the fire-extinguishing arm which is consumer-side.
  - *Dwarven Thrower*: returns-on-throw + type-conditional damage. The on-throw return is a new shape (the engine has no throw-return primitive); compose-wise it's a "after-throw, item teleports back to wielder's hand" hook on the thrown-weapon attack path. The vs-Giants damage rider shares the type-conditional shape with Holy Avenger / Sun Blade.
  - *Hammer of Thunderbolts*: ability-score-floor enchantment plus a save-or-die on thunder crits. The "STR becomes 27 when wielding the hammer with belt and gauntlets equipped" arm wants an extension of the items-batch-1.2 `OverrideAbilityScore` primitive that gates the override on a multi-item attunement set (predicate: all three items attuned by the wearer). The save-or-die-on-thunder-crit arm composes the new `WeaponCritRider` from Vicious / Vorpal with a CON-save outcome.
  - *Berserker Axe*: item-curse-triggered compulsion plus level-scaled HP boost. The HP-boost ("gain `level` max HP while attuned") wants a `ModifyMaxHP { formula: 'level' }` projection effect — clean wiring once projection lands. The frenzy-curse-on-damage-in-combat arm needs a brand-new item-curse primitive: the engine doesn't model "item the wearer can't voluntarily release" or "compulsion to attack the nearest visible creature on your turn" (the closest neighbor is `ApplyCondition` with charmed / frightened, but compulsion-to-attack-nearest is its own RAW shape). Defer the curse arm; ship the HP-boost in the projection wave.
  - *Attunement-restriction enforcement*: Holy Avenger requires a Paladin; Dwarven Thrower requires a Dwarf. The schema's `attunementCondition: string` field is in place (used by Pearl of Power for "Spellcaster"); the engine's attunement reducer doesn't currently parse this string into a class / species predicate, so the restriction is data-only today. Could wire as a free-text matcher against the bearer's `classId` / `speciesId` once an attunement-precondition gate lands on the attunement planner.
- **Items-batch-1.10 named armors and shields** (the defensive parallel to batch 1.9). All eight ship as `itemKind: 'magic'` with `effects: []` and no charges, matching the existing Cloak of Protection and Armor +1 / +2 / +3 shape (consumer-side overlays the magic-item template onto a base armor or shield, identical pattern to the weapon templates). Cross-cutting blocker remains the magic-item-effect-projection pass plus the slice-76 `ItemInstance.temporaryBuff` extension into a permanent / inherent counterpart that batch 1.5 named for the +N templates. Per-item shapes this batch introduces:
  - *Adamantine Armor*: inbound-attack-crit-rewrite primitive. RAW says any crit against the wearer becomes a normal hit while the armor is worn. The damage-resolution chain doesn't currently expose a "convert crit to normal hit" hook the way armor / item effects could intercept. Composes as `RewriteIncomingCrit` on the wearer's effect stack once projection lands, with the resolver consulting the flag before applying the crit-multiplier branch.
  - *Mithral Armor*: armor-property-suppression. RAW removes both stealth disadvantage and any strength requirement from the underlying medium / heavy armor. Straightforward predicate-modification on the existing `stealthDisadvantage` and `strRequirement` armor fields once projection lands; no new primitive needed.
  - *Elven Chain*: armor-category-override primitive. RAW: counts as light armor for the wearer (so e.g. Stealth checks aren't disadvantaged, DEX cap is removed for AC). Needs a `ReclassifyArmor { newCategory: 'light' }` effect that overrides the base armor's category in the AC and proficiency-check derivations. New primitive, single user.
  - *Glamoured Studded Leather*: bonus-action appearance change. Pure narrative (the wearer chooses an appearance the armor can mimic). Composes with a future `UseItem` planner for the activation step, but the cosmetic-change result is consumer-side; defer entirely to narrative.
  - *Dragon Scale Mail*: resistance-by-dragon-color plus the +1 AC bonus the entry also carries. The AC bonus composes with `AddModifier { target: 'ac', value: 1 }` once projection lands. The resistance arm wants `GrantResistance { damageType }` where the damage type is chosen at item-instantiation time per the dragon color the scales came from (acid for black, lightning for blue, cold for white, fire for red, etc.); needs an item-instance "variant" field the engine doesn't currently model, or a per-instance configuration that the consumer fills in. Defer the variant arm; the AC bonus wires for free.
  - *Demon Armor*: item-curse plus offensive-claw grant. The cursed-and-can't-doff-voluntarily arm composes with the Berserker Axe item-curse primitive from batch 1.9. The "fiendish claws" arm is novel: the armor grants the wearer a natural-weapon attack (claws dealing 1d8 + STR slashing). Needs a `GrantNaturalAttack { name, dice, damageType, ability }` effect kind the engine doesn't model (closest is a planner that creates a virtual weapon instance and adds it to the wearer's available attack list). Single user today but generalizes to monk unarmed strikes, drake breath weapons, and tabaxi claws across content yet to ship.
  - *Sentinel Shield*: ✓ wired in slice 139. Both arms ship as `SetAdvantage` entries (`on: 'initiative'` + `on: { kind: 'skill', skill: 'perception' }`). No deferred sub-arms.
  - *Spellguard Shield*: composite save-and-attack defense. Advantage on saving throws against spells (composes with `SetAdvantage { on: 'save', savedAgainst: 'spell' }`; needs a "save against spell" predicate the existing save shape doesn't carry). Plus: spell attacks against the wearer have disadvantage (needs an inbound-attack-disadvantage primitive scoped to spell attacks specifically, related to but distinct from the conditional-disadvantage shape Cloak of Elvenkind wants). Two new sub-predicates; same projection blocker as the rest.
