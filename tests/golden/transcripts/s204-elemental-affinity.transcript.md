# Elemental Affinity (Fire): +CHA-mod on fire-bolt damage

**Ember** joined (sorcerer 6, 38/38 HP).

**Goblin** joined (fighter 1, 40/40 HP).

Choice required for **Ember**: Choose your draconic damage type. (Fire).

**Ember** chose: fire.

**Ember** casts Fire Bolt (cantrip) at Goblin.

**Ember** attacks **Goblin**: d20(14) + 7 = 21 vs AC 9 -> hit.

Damage rolled: 1d10=[2,2]+4 fire.

**Goblin** takes 8 damage from **Ember** (8 fire). (HP 40 -> 32)
