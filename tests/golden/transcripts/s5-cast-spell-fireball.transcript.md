# Wizard casts Fireball at two goblins

**Mage** joined (wizard 5, 28/28 HP).

**Goblin A** joined (fighter 1, 30/30 HP).

**Goblin B** joined (fighter 1, 30/30 HP).

**Mage** casts Fireball (3rd-level slot) at Goblin A, Goblin B.

Slot consumed: 3rd-level.

**Goblin A** DEX save: d20(11) + 2 = 13 vs DC 15 -> failure.

**Goblin A** takes 24 damage from **Mage** (24 fire). (HP 30 -> 6)

**Goblin B** DEX save: d20(1) + 2 = 3 vs DC 15 -> failure.

**Goblin B** takes 24 damage from **Mage** (24 fire). (HP 30 -> 6)
