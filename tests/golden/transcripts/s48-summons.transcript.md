# Find Familiar + Summon Beast; concentration breaks

**Alyx** joined (wizard 5, 28/28 HP).

**Alyx** casts Find Familiar (1st-level slot) at no targets.

Slot consumed: 1st-level.

Alyx summons **Familiar** (10 HP, AC 13) via find-familiar at slot 1.

**Alyx** casts Summon Beast (2nd-level slot) at no targets.

Slot consumed: 2nd-level.

Alyx summons **Bestial Spirit** (30 HP, AC 11) via summon-beast at slot 2.

**Alyx** is now concentrating on Summon Beast.

**Alyx**'s concentration on Summon Beast broke (damage).

Familiar is dismissed.
